TUTORIAL VIDEO

LOGIN PAGE:

  • First, log in using your username and password by clicking the "ENTER" button and turn on your location before login a app

GALLERY PAGE:

  • On the gallery screen, tap the top "Take Photo" button to open the camera page and capture a photo.
  • After capturing a photo, it will appear as a pending entry on the gallery page. Tap the ">" (arrow) to fill in the required details.
  • At the bottom of the gallery screen, there are three buttons:
  • Click the "Copy Link" button to copy the RM Camera link.
  • Click the "Link Photos" button to navigate to the "Sample Pics" page showing RM photos.
  • Click the "RM-History" button to navigate to the "History Page", which shows your submitted details.
  • Submitted entries appear in green, while pending entries appear in light red.

CAMERA PAGE:

  • On the camera page, there is a button to open the camera, with options for both front and back cameras.
  • Tap the camera button to take photos.
  • You can only submit your photos after taking at least one; the submit button will be disabled otherwise.
  • Once you submit your photos, you will be redirected to the gallery page to edit your form.

EDIT FORM PAGE:

  • On the edit form page, update your information along with the attached photos.
  • The submit button will not be visible until the first six data value cards are filled out.
  • After filling in all the fields, click the submit button to complete the process.

RM IMAGES PAGE:

  • On the RM History page, you will see all images taken through the provided link.
  • Select one or multiple photos, then press the "ADD" button to add them to the edit form.

RM HISTORY PAGE:

  • On the RM History page, you will see all the details you have submitted.

CHANGE PASSWORD PAGE:

  • On the Change Password page, enter your registered Name, current password, and new password. Confirm the new password, then click the "Update Password" button.
  • Your password will be successfully updated.
